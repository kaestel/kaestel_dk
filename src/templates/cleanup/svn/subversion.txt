

No Auth:

<Location /svn>
  DAV svn
   SVNParentPath //Volumes/FIREWIRE200/repositories
</Location>

HTTP Basic Auth:

<Location /svn>
   DAV svn
   SVNParentPath //Volumes/FIREWIRE200/repositories
   AuthType Basic
   AuthName "Subversion repository"
   AuthUserFile //Volumes/FIREWIRE40/svn-auth-file
   Require valid-user
</Location>

Create httpasswd:
htpasswd -cm //Volumes/FIREWIRE40/svn-auth-file__USERNAME__

htpasswd -cm /usr/local/apache-auth-file__USERNAME__

htpasswd -m /usr/local/apache-auth-file__USERNAME__
