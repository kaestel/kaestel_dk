My self-summary
I'm the oddest of the evens.

I'm part time revolutionary. I spend the other part serving the system I revolt against. Surely, one day this will lead to a breakdown. I wonder who will win.

I don't follow rules - it tends to get really boring. I don't mind guidelines - occasionally they can be helpful. I like to challenge conventions in search for better alternatives.

I think principles are only good for discussions. In real life you need to be able to adapt. Quickly. Or get stuck in a defense you might not even want to defend. It happens. Moral and ethics are much deeper than principles, and should be respected, at least to avoid bad conscience.

What I'm really looking for
Yeah, ok, I know this is not an official section of the profile, but I'm looking for more than just a perfect pattern-match. Actually a good relationship probably requires its differences. Differences help us grow as human beings. They add perspective and I find that essential. With that said, we probably won't get along, if you think the world is just fine and dandy in its current state.

I am uncompromisingly focused on my personal projects. They involve rethinking every aspect of our society, challenging every norm and finally providing a credible and achievable alternative to our current way of living. For this reason, I might not be suited for a "normal" relationship. But I still dream. I have a lot to give, if you pay attention.

I want to find someone who is willing to help me change the world, or at least try. Someone who believes we can do so much more than what we've been schooled to believe. Know the truth is always deeper, than what we are told.

You can be quiet or loud as long as you shine. But please shine. We can all shine, if we just accept the color of our glow.

Someone with their feet on the ground and their head in the clouds. Maybe someone who knows, we are both weak and strong. In different areas, at different times.

If you think I am full of shit. Look in the mirror. Get a job, one you like.
